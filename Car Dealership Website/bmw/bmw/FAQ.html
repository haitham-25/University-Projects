How can I charge my BMW electric vehicle at home?

There are two ways for home charging:
- BMW Wallbox
- The charger supplied with the car

Where can I find the electric charging station in public?

1. Through INFINITY app (BMW Egypt charging partner).
2. Normally, INFINITY stations are found in most of the gas stations all around Cairo and travel roads.

What requirements do I need to install a BMW Wallbox for the BMW fast charger at home?

The customers needs to ensure having 3-phase power supply at his home, then INFINITY (BMW Egypt charging partner) takes care of the installation process.
