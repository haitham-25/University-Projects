WE ARE M

On our 50th anniversary we're celebrating five decades of fascinating history with a great community. We dedicate this jubilee not only to our numerous successes, but above all to our enthusiastic fans, drivers and all M enthusiasts A new era is beginning, an era of emotion, electrification and powerful technologies. LEGENDARY MOTORSPORT LOGO.

The start of the anniversary year sees the return of the iconic BMW Motorsport emblem.
BMW M is putting down a marker for those who love tradition.
The logo with the staggered semicircles in blue, purple and red was first used on race cars of BMW Motorsport GmbH, founded in 1972.
All BMW M models and all BMW models with M Sport package now have the option of having the classic logo on the front, back and wheel hubs.


COLOURS OF M.
