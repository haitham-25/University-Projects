BMW iX1 - Long Wheelbase

The fully electric BMW iX1 eDrive20 radiates electrifying determination. Its functionality and versatility inspire new paths in everyday life:
  • Bold exterior design using monolithic lines and distinctive proportions.

  • Exceptional interior comfort with active massage seats and lumbar support.

  • Modern multimedia cockpit with BMW Curved Display and voice control.

  • Full electric flexibility with a range of up to 474 km.

BMW iX1 eDrive20 Specifications:

Energy consumption (WLTP, combined): 17.2–15.4 kWh/100 km
Electric range (WLTP): 430–474 km

EXTERIOR HIGHLIGHTS OF THE BMW iX1.

Front Design

The distinctive and large, almost square double kidney grille and the slim Adaptive LED headlights,
extending far into the flanks, give the BMW iX1 a powerful and electrifying presence.

Side view

With its prominent front section and upright proportions,
the BMW iX1 presents an extremely dynamic image in the side view.
Its exterior is characterised by short overhangs and a long wheelbase typical of a Sports Activity Vehicle.

Rear view.

Thanks to their interplay of transparent and dark smoked glass together with the hexagonal pattern[1] in the side,
the three-dimensional L-shaped rear lights deliver an unmistakably modern statement.

BMW CHARGING: EASY. ANYTIME. EVERYWHERE.

Charging at home.

With the Flexible Fast Charger,
your BMW iX1 already offers everything that you need for convenient charging at home right from the time of delivery.
If you wish, you can further adapt the charging options to your own needs.

Charging on the move.

With BMW Charging Card, you can charge throughout Egypt in one of the largest and constantly expanding public charging networks.
This includes everything you need for public charging in Egypt,
with the BMW Charging Card and the charging cable included in the standard of supply for your BMW.

BMW Wallbox.

With the BMW Wallbox provided with the BMW iX1
you can charge conveniently in the comfort of your home with 22 kW of charging power.
