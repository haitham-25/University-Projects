Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AutoDrive

How do I book a test drive? +

Booking a test drive is easy! Simply browse our car listings, find a vehicle you're interested in, and click the "Book Test Drive" button. Fill out the form with your preferred date, time, and dealership, and we'll confirm your appointment.

Is there a fee for using AutoDrive? +

No! AutoDrive is completely free for car buyers. You can browse listings, compare vehicles, book test drives, and use all our tools at no cost.

Can I compare multiple cars? +

Yes! Our comparison tool allows you to compare up to 3 vehicles side-by-side. Simply add cars to your comparison list and view detailed specs, features, and pricing all in one place.

How accurate are the prices listed? +

All prices are provided directly by dealerships and updated regularly. However, final pricing may vary based on location, trim level, options, and current promotions. We recommend contacting the dealership for the most current pricing.

Do you offer financing? +

While AutoDrive doesn't directly provide financing, we offer a loan calculator to estimate monthly payments and connect you with dealerships that offer various financing options.

Can I save my favorite cars? +

Absolutely! Click the heart icon on any car listing to save it to your favorites. You can access your saved cars anytime from your dashboard.

What if I need help during my car search? +

Our support team is here to help! You can reach us via email at info@autodrive.com or call 1-800-AUTO-DRIVE during business hours. You can also visit any of our partner dealerships for in-person assistance.

Can I trade in my current vehicle? +

Yes! Most of our partner dealerships accept trade-ins. Contact the dealership directly or mention your trade-in when booking a test drive to discuss valuation options.
